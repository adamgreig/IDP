Handle arriving at a junction, either the target junction or an intermediate one where a turn may be required. 

If we are at the target junction, return with NAVIGATION\_\-ARRIVED, or at an intermediate junction look up the required action and then tell \hyperlink{classIDP_1_1LineFollowing}{LineFollowing} whether to go straight forward or turn, and keep calling Navigation::turn() as appropriate to execute the turn.

\begin{DoxyReturn}{Returns}
NAVIGATION\_\-ARRIVED if at the target junction, or NAVIGATION\_\-ENROUTE otherwise. 
\end{DoxyReturn}
