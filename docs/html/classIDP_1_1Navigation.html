NavigationStatus IDP::Navigation::handle_junction ( const NavigationNode  target  )  [private]

Handle arriving at a junction, either the target junction or an intermediate one where a turn may be required.

If we are at the target junction, return with NAVIGATION_ARRIVED, or at an intermediate junction look up the required action and then tell LineFollowing whether to go straight forward or turn, and keep calling Navigation::turn() as appropriate to execute the turn.

Returns:
NAVIGATION_ARRIVED if at the target junction, or NAVIGATION_ENROUTE otherwise.
